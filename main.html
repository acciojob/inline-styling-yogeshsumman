Flowers photos

Flowers are the reproductive structures of flowering plants. They come in various colors, shapes, and sizes, serving both aesthetic and ecological purposes. Many flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies with their scent and nectar.

Roses pictures

Roses are woody perennial flowering plants of the genus Rosa. Known as the "Queen of Flowers," they come in colors like red, white, pink, and yellow. Roses are often used to symbolize love, affection, and appreciation.
